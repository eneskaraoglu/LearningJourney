# Exercise - Flutter Performance and Rebuild Discipline

## Part A - Core Implementation
Take a moderately complex screen and identify obvious rebuild, list, or layout inefficiencies.

## Part B - Validation and Error Cases
Test performance under large lists, rapid interaction, and slower device conditions.

## Part C - Reliability and Refactor
Refactor expensive work out of hot UI paths and compare before and after behavior.

## Constraints
- Measure or observe before changing code.
- Keep readability intact while optimizing.
- Focus on meaningful bottlenecks.

## Acceptance Criteria
- At least one real performance issue is reduced.
- The refactor does not make the codebase harder to understand.
- Results are validated on a realistic scenario.

## Bonus Challenge
Document a simple performance checklist for future screens.

## Reflection Prompts
- Which performance assumption turned out wrong?
- What optimization had the best payoff for the least complexity?
