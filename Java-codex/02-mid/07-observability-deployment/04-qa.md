# Q&A

Q: What is MDC?
A: Mapped diagnostic context for per-request log fields.

Q: Why use Actuator?
A: It provides health, metrics, and diagnostics endpoints.

Q: What is a readiness probe?
A: Indicates whether a service is ready to receive traffic.

Q: How do traces help?
A: They show end-to-end request paths and latencies.

Q: Why structured logs?
A: They are easier to search and analyze.

Q: What is graceful shutdown?
A: Allowing in-flight requests to complete before stopping.

Q: What is a canary deployment?
A: Releasing to a small subset of traffic before full rollout.

Q: How do you monitor latency?
A: Track p95 and p99 metrics with alerts.
