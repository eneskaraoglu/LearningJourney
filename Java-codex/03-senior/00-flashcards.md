# Senior Flashcards

Q: What is a bounded context?
A: A domain boundary with consistent models and language.

Q: What is a saga?
A: A sequence of local transactions with compensations.

Q: CAP tradeoff in partitions?
A: Choose consistency or availability.

Q: What is tail latency?
A: High percentiles like p95/p99, not averages.

Q: What is an error budget?
A: Allowed downtime derived from SLOs.

Q: Why use circuit breakers?
A: To stop repeated calls to failing services.

Q: What is CQRS?
A: Separate read and write models.

Q: What is zero-downtime deployment?
A: Deploy without service interruption, often via blue-green or canary.
