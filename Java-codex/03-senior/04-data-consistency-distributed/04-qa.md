# Q&A

Q: What does CAP mean?
A: In partitions, you choose between consistency and availability.

Q: What is a saga?
A: A sequence of local transactions with compensations.

Q: Choreography vs orchestration?
A: Choreography is event-driven; orchestration is centralized control.

Q: What is eventual consistency?
A: Data becomes consistent over time, not instantly.

Q: Why use an outbox?
A: To avoid losing events between DB and message broker.

Q: What is CQRS?
A: Separate models for reads and writes.

Q: What is idempotency in distributed systems?
A: Processing the same event multiple times safely.

Q: What is a compensating action?
A: An undo step to restore consistency.
