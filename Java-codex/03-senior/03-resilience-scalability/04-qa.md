# Q&A

Q: Resilience vs scalability?
A: Resilience handles failure; scalability handles growth.

Q: What is backpressure?
A: Signaling upstream systems to slow down.

Q: Why use bulkheads?
A: To prevent one failure from consuming all resources.

Q: What is jitter in retries?
A: Random delay to avoid retry storms.

Q: What are SLIs and SLOs?
A: Indicators and objectives for reliability targets.

Q: What is a rate limiter?
A: A mechanism to cap request rate per client.

Q: Why avoid retries on non-idempotent calls?
A: They can cause duplicate side effects.

Q: What is graceful degradation?
A: Serving partial functionality under stress.
